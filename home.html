WELCOME

Computer science is the foundation of the information technology era that we are living in. Therefore, if society is to make rapid economic advancements and fully benefit from information technology, computer science education is essential. The study and creation of applications that enable us to use computers on a daily basis, the software systems that underpin these applications, and the techniques and procedures employed to create applications and systems that function dependably, accurately, and morally are all included in computer science. Employers who hire computer scientists expect competent programmers with knowledge of modern techniques and tools as well as the ability to apply sound engineering principles to the development, construction, and maintenance of their products. Computer programming is at the core of computer science.

ABOUT US

Welcome to the University of Ghana Computer Science Department!

At the forefront of innovation and academic excellence, the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana is a dynamic hub for aspiring technologists and researchers.
Our department is committed to shaping the future of technology through cutting-edge education, research, and practical skill development.

Crafting Brilliance

At the heart of our excellence lie these key features, meticulously crafted for your success.



Comprehensive Programs

Offering a range of undergraduate and graduate programs, including specialized areas
such as Actuarial Science, Biomathematics, Information Technology, and more.
Our curriculum is designed to equip students with a solid foundation in computer science principles
and hands-on experience.

Diverse Research Tools

Students and faculty benefit from access to robust research tools, including the renowned Balme
Library, online databases like IEEE Xplore and
ACM Digital Library, and engaging virtual labs
such as Codecademy and GitHub.

Global Perspective

With an international admissions pathway, we welcome students from around the world,
fostering a diverse and enriching academic environment. Our commitment to global
perspectives prepares students for success in the interconnected world of technology.

Expert Faculty

Our dedicated faculty, experts in their fields,
guide students through a curriculum that reflects
the latest industry trends. Small class sizes ensure personalized attention, fostering a supportive
learning environment.

Innovation and Collaboration

The department encourages innovation and collaboration, providing students with
opportunities to engage in real-world projects, internships, and collaborative coding efforts.
Our aim is to produce graduates who are not only proficient in theory but also adept at applying their knowledge to solve complex problems.
