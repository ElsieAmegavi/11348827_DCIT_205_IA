Student Resources: Internships and Scholarships

At the University of Ghana Computer Science Department, we are committed to providing students with valuable resources to enhance their academic journey.
Explore the opportunities below to enrich your experience and invest in your future:

Internships:

Unlock Real-World Experience

Our department facilitates internship programs that bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application.
Partnering with leading industry players, students have the chance to gain hands-on experience, build professional networks, and apply their skills in authentic settings.

Key Features:

  • Collaborations with Industry Leaders
  • Practical Application of Classroom Knowledge
  • Networking Opportunities
  • Skill Development in a Professional Environment

Investing in Your Success

We understand the financial aspects of education and are dedicated to supporting deserving students.
The Computer Science Department offers a range of scholarships, recognizing academic excellence, leadership, and innovative contributions to the field.

Available Schorlarships

  • Academic Excellence Scholarships
  • Leadership and Innovation Awards
  • Need-Based Scholarships

Application Process:

Details on scholarship applications, eligibility criteria, and deadlines are available through the University of Ghana's Scholarship Office

Navigating Opportunities

One can also apply for schorlaships to other countries with help from the University. Visit the Campus France Ghana website for more information or visit their office on campus. They have several opportunities for comuter science students.
Our commitment is to empower students to thrive academically, professionally, and financially. For more information on internships, explore our Internship Program page, and to discover scholarship opportunities, visit the Scholarship Office on the University's website.
At the University of Ghana, we believe in investing in your potential and providing resources that propel you toward success. Your journey to a rewarding and impactful career in computer science starts here!
